Cutty Sark

The blend that started the Jazz Age Charge of the Light Brigade hasn’t lost any of its bright-young-thing charms. Cutty is about a porcelain-light coolness: blanched almond, lemon cheesecake, vanilla, balanced by almost hidden silky depths. No surprise that it works brilliantly with soda.
